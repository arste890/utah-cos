The Mentoring

Communication Agreement

The College of Science requires mentors and mentees to complete a communication agreement. This is not required until the start of the semester; however, we can complete it prior to the start of the semester if you would like. In order to complete our communication agreement, the College of Science requires a "face-to-face conversation," so we can complete this over Zoom (or through a similar platform) or in person; I will let you decide, and I will be happy to facilitate either option. 
 

What is on the Communication Agreement?

The agreement covers:
  • Communication Frequency and Modality
  • Goals for the Program
  • Boundaries
  • An Agreement of Respect and Confidentiality
